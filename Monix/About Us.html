Mission

To advocate for and promote gender equality by challenging societal norms, eliminating gender-based discrimination and empowering individuals of all genders
To create a more inclusive society where everyone regardless of gender has equal access to opportunities, resources and rights.

Vision

Our vision is a world where gender equality is a reality, where individuals of all genders can thrive without facing barriers or biases and where equal opportunities and respect are fundamental rights for everyone
We strive for a society where diversity is celebrated and everyone can contribute to their fullest potential.

Core Values

  • Empowerment
  • Adovancy for Justice
  • Accountability
  • Innovation
